About Comparatea

What is
Comparatea

Comparatea makes comparing products on different websites a cup of 'tea'. Hence the name Comparatea. Using BeautifulSoup(bs4) we scrape flipkart.com and amazon.in and display the cheapest product available on both sites along with name, picture and link. We are using bubble sorting algorithm to sort product according to the prices. Flask(Python library) is used as backend and simple Html and CSS as frontend.
